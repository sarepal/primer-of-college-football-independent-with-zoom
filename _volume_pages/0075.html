{% raw %}8. Tackling.— The object in breaking{% endraw %}
{% raw %}through is to tackle the runner behind{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his own line. Once let him reach the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}line, and he is bound to gain something.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Tackle him behind the line, before his in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}terference can get formed and well start¬{% endraw %}
