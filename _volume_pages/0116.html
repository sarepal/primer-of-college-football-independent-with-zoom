{% raw %}If your opponent exposes enough of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}chest, give him a hard push back into the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}centre; this will leave the guard free to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}back up tackle.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}your man, lift him to his feet with your{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shoulder, and then straighten arms on{% endraw %}
{% raw %}him, twisting him back into the centre.{% endraw %}
