{% raw %}Whether on the offence or the defence,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the three backs should play together as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}much as possible, so as to understand each{% endraw %}
{% raw %}other and how best to help each other.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The backs should play in the same position{% endraw %}
