{% raw %}A quarter should remember that his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}work is not done simply by giving the ball{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the back, but he must get into the in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}terference and the push. When the ball{% endraw %}

{% raw %}quarter should follow up his pass, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the moment the ball leaves his hand turn{% endraw %}

{% raw %}ous forward coming through the line to{% endraw %}
